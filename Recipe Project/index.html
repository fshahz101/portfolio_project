Chicken Karahi

Savor the bold flavors of Chicken Karachi, a hearty stew infused with a blend of aromatic spices and tender chicken pieces, simmered to perfection in a rich, tomato-based gravy. This Pakistani-inspired dish promises a deliciously spicy kick with every bite.


Aloo Paratha

Aloo Paratha, a North Indian staple, is a nourishing flatbread stuffed with a spiced mixture of mashed potatoes, then pan-fried to a golden crisp, offering a satisfying blend of comfort and flavor in every bite.
